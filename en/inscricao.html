WER 2026 Registration

The author must register for each accepted article.

The required information is:

  • Personal information
    • Name
    • Surname
    • Email
    • Affiliation
    • Nationality
    • Role: Author or Assistant
    • Identification: DNI or passport
  • Billing information
    • Bill to (University, Company, Individual)
    • Payer's Address (City, ZIP Code, State, Country)
    • CUIT/CUIL number (Argentina) / Tax ID number (foreign)
    • Any specific notes to include on the bill (optional)
  • If you are the author
    • Paper ID
    • Paper title
    • Track: Research track, Software Requirements Tools track, Industry track, Master’s and Doctoral track, Student track
