Solicitação de Patrocinador para a Conferência Internacional WER 2024

The WER International Requirements Engineering Congress began in 1998 as a Requirements Engineering Workshop and has been held annually ever since in different cities in the Americas and the Iberian Peninsula. WER is looking for other cities around the world to host its next events.

WER is the best venue to present and discuss progress in research, software development, international standards, and software tools and their applications within the field of Requirements Engineering (RE), as well as future trends such as AI and RE, Data Science and RE, and Robotics and RE, among others.

WER 2026 offers a high-quality program consisting of research sessions, mini-tutorials, an industry panel, doctoral and master's sessions, student posters, industry product demonstrations, case study presentations for professionals, and high-profile international speakers.

WER 2026 will be an in-person conference featuring live streaming of keynote presentations and a special space to enrich discussions among participants.

In 2026, the 29th edition will be held in the city of La Plata, hosted by the University of La Plata. This new edition is organized by a Local Organizing Committee comprising Dr. Graciela Hadad (UB and UNO), Paula Angeleri, MSc (UB, UNLaM, and UTN), and Dr. Leandro Antonelli (UNLP and UAI), as well as a distinguished International Academic Committee.

Thank you for supporting us through sponsorship! We look forward to seeing you at the University of La Plata in Buenos Aires, Argentina!
