WER 2026

WER is an important forum for discussion of relevant issues in the area of Requirements Engineering. The workshop has been held annually since 1998, alternating among Latin American countries, Canada, Spain, and Portugal.

WER is open to the submission of unpublished papers on research, teaching, and industry experience in the field of Requirements Engineering. Although the workshop is based on scientific papers, it provides a variety of tracks, activities, and presentations, as well as opportunities for valuable interactions and networking among participants. WER proceedings are DBLP-indexed and published under ISBN. Additionally, papers can be accessed at the WERpapers repository.

In 2026, in its twenty-ninth edition, WER will be held in La Plata, Argentina.

The event is held simultaneously with the Argentine Informatics Conference (55 JAIIO).

For more information, contact organizacao.wer@gmail.com.
