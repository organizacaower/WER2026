Instructions for Authors

All papers must present original findings, be written in Portuguese, Spanish, or English, and be submitted in PDF format via EasyChair, following the LNCS formatting guidelines.

The review process is double-blind (except for Journal First track). All submissions (and supplementary materials) must be properly anonymized according to the following guidelines:

  • The names and affiliations of authors must not be visible anywhere in the paper.
  • Acknowledgments must be anonymized or removed for the review process.
  • Self-citations should be included when necessary but must use third-person phrasing. For example, the phrase "as shown in our previous study [2]" is not allowed, whereas "as shown in Smith et al. [2]" is acceptable.
  • Logos and other institutional identifiers in figures must be blurred or removed.

Please follow these guidelines to avoid having submissions rejected without review.

Any paper that exceeds the page limits, is out of scope, or does not follow the formatting guidelines will be rejected without review.

All submissions will be reviewed by at least three Program Committee members.

All accepted papers will be published with open access through the WERpapers repository.
