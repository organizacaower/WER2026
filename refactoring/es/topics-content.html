Topics of Interest

  • Foundations and Processes of Requirements Engineering

    Elicitation, analysis, specification, modeling, management, traceability, prioritization, negotiation, verification, and validation.

  • Methods, Techniques, and Tools

    Languages, processes, tools, DSLs, metamodeling, and automated support for requirements specification and management.

  • Model-Based Requirements Engineering

    Model-driven engineering, requirements modeling, and model-based approaches.

  • Development Paradigms and Approaches

    Requirements in agent-oriented, service-oriented, aspect-oriented, ecosystem, system-of-systems, and industrial standards environments.

  • Requirements Engineering in Modern Methodologies

    Agile, DevOps, and requirements management in distributed and dynamic settings.

  • Emerging Domains and Systems

    Mobile and web systems, critical, legal, or military systems, sustainability, cyber-physical systems, IoT, ubiquitous computing, and extended reality.

  • Artificial Intelligence Applied to Requirements Engineering

    AI and large language models for elicitation, analysis, validation, automation, and intelligent assistants.

  • Human, Organizational, and Educational Aspects

    Education and training, stakeholder collaboration, and human factors in requirements engineering.

  • Regulatory Compliance and Legal Aspects

    Regulations, certifications, standards, compliance, and intellectual property.
